Test page for diagrams

Pintora is a text-to-diagrams library. An official life editor to test can be found here. Also in this test page, you can test diagrams. Just use the textarea to write the structure, then click the button to display the resulting diagram.

Bug report:
For some reason, the standalone version of Pintora deletes the textarea from this page when drawing a diagram. After an update of Pintora, I will delete this notification.

Side note:
The name Pintora comes from Spanish word for 'female painter'. In view of various cross-cultural influences, the word could also be an English-Egyptian hybrid of 'pin to Ra'. A phrase for believers in a supreme god like the Egyptian Ra, 'connecting people' as a famous slogan about Nokia phones goes.
